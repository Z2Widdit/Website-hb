Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026 · Applies to fivestarrcust0mz.vercel.app and related FiveStarrCust0mz web pages

1. Who we are

This website is operated by FiveStarrCust0mz (“we”, “us”, “our”), a creator project offering game mods, community links, and related content. This Privacy Policy explains how we process personal data when you visit or use this website.

For privacy questions or requests under applicable data protection law (including the EU/UK GDPR where it applies), contact us via our Discord server or open a support ticket there.

2. Scope

This policy covers data processed through this website (including the home page, photography page, and mod-request form). Third-party platforms we link to (for example Patreon, Ko-fi, ModLand, YouTube, TikTok, or Discord) have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for how those services handle your data once you leave our site.

3. What data we process

Depending on how you use the site, we may process:

  • Technical / hosting data: IP address, request timestamps, browser user-agent, and similar connection metadata processed automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel) for security, reliability, and abuse prevention.
  • Device preferences stored locally: theme choice (fsc-theme) and optional favorite-mod list (fsc_favs) saved in your browser localStorage only. These stay on your device and are not sent to our servers as part of normal browsing.
  • Mod request form data: if you submit a request, we process the game/platform, your message, and any Discord username you choose to provide. Submissions are delivered to our private Discord channel via a server-side webhook so we can respond.
  • Discord community widget: if you view the embedded Discord widget, Discord may process technical data according to Discord’s Privacy Policy.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data (for example health or biometric data) through this website.

4. Why we process data (purposes & legal bases)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on the following bases:

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): operating and securing the website, preventing spam/abuse on the request form, remembering theme preference on your device, and understanding basic hosting reliability.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): voluntary form fields you choose to fill (for example Discord username) and any optional features you actively enable.
  • Contract / pre-contract steps (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): handling a mod or commission request you submit so we can follow up.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

This website does not set first-party marketing or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage only for appearance (light/dark theme) and optional favorites. You can clear this data anytime in your browser settings.

Third-party services (for example Google Fonts, Font Awesome CDN, Discord widgets, or linked platforms) may set their own cookies or similar technologies under their policies when their content loads.

6. Who we share data with

We share data only as needed to run the site:

  • Vercel Inc. — website hosting and edge delivery (server logs / infrastructure).
  • Discord Inc. — community widget and delivery of mod-request messages to our server/channel.
  • Content / CDN providers used to load fonts, icons, or media (for example Google Fonts, Cloudflare CDN for Font Awesome, and image hosts referenced on the site).

We do not sell your personal data.

7. International transfers

Our hosting and some third-party providers may process data in the United States or other countries outside your own. Where required, those providers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) described in their privacy documentation.

8. How long we keep data

  • Hosting logs: retained by the host according to their standard operational retention (typically short-term for security and debugging).
  • Mod requests: kept in our Discord workspace as long as needed to handle your request and for reasonable follow-up, then removed or archived when no longer necessary.
  • localStorage data: remains on your device until you clear site data or change preferences.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us on Discord with enough detail for us to verify and respond to your request. We will respond within a reasonable time and as required by law.

10. Children

This site is aimed at a general gaming audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum digital consent age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through the form, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Security

We use HTTPS, security headers (including Content-Security-Policy), and server-side handling for form submissions so secrets are not exposed in the browser. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please avoid sending highly sensitive personal information through the public request form.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after an update means you acknowledge the revised policy.
